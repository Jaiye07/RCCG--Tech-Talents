About us

The RCCG Tech Talent

RCCG Tech Talents is a non-profit organization and we are committed to making Nigeria the country with the highest percentage of youth in the technology workforce.

We are embracing the technology of the future and advancing youth in technology from the bootcamp to the workspace.

Aim

Our aim is to contribute to the fast-growing tech industry. Technology is the language of the future, and every youth should learn it. RCCG Tech Talents mission is to close the

unemployment gap and to help the youth embrace technology of the future today. We help youth with the ability to access decent job opportunities within the future workplace.

Mission

Our mission is to create a vibrant tech community and provide mentorship, training, and visibility for tech enthusiasts of all levels. Join us in shaping the next generation of tech leaders

  • To build a community that embodies technical growth, networking, and mentorship
  • Nurturing today’s talent, creating tomorrow’s opportunities.
  • Working together to nurture tech talent in Africa.
  • Ensuring greater access to digital opportunities
  • To have a member of RCCG Tech Talent in every family of all nations.
